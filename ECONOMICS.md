# ECONOMICS

## Funnel assumptions (example)
- 1,000 visitors/month
- 25% start audit (250)
- 60% finish audit (150)
- 20% create share link (30)
- 15% submit email (22–25 leads)

## Lead value
- Define 2 lead tiers:
  - High-intent: savings > $500/mo (qualified for consult)
  - Normal: savings <= $500/mo

Example assumptions:
- High-intent leads close at 10% into a paid credits deal.
- Average gross margin per deal: $3,000.

If 20% of leads are high-intent:
- 25 total leads/month → 5 high-intent
- 10% close → 0.5 deals/month
- Expected gross margin ≈ $1,500/month

## CAC target
If expected gross margin is $1,500/month at current volume, CAC should be kept low via:
- organic distribution
- community posts
- founder-led outbound

## LLM cost
Narrative summaries are generated on-demand and cached.
- Assume $0.01–$0.05 per summary (varies by model).
- At 150 finished audits/mo with 30 narrative generations, cost is manageable.

## Notes
These are planning numbers for the MVP; instrumentation in `METRICS.md` is needed to validate.
